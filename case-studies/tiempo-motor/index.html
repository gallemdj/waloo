Case studies

Tiempo Motor

Automotive news publisher—from a slow blog-style WordPress site to a curated editorial platform, custom theme, dedicated infrastructure, and full ongoing operations.

Client & project introduction

Focus
WordPress · Performance · Hosting
Client
Tiempo Motor
Year
2018

Tiempo Motor is Argentina’s leading automotive news brand—daily coverage of launches, industry, business, and test drives. This case follows the engagement from legacy WordPress constraints through a custom rebuild, platform migration, and measurable gains in speed, reach, and discoverability.

Before

What we received

The client came to us with a WordPress site that performed poorly, offered a limited design, and could not support the editorial and commercial model they needed for the national automotive market.

Publishing was essentially chronological blog flow: the homepage could not be curated, internal pages offered little structure for featured coverage, and the stack was not built for the traffic or monetization goals ahead.

In production, the homepage took about 25 seconds to load—unusable for a daily news brand. Lighthouse-style performance on desktop sat around 50; lab timings on the legacy stack showed DOMContentLoaded near 11 seconds and full page load above 11.5 seconds.

Proposal

Editorial product + platform

We proposed a full rebuild—not a skin on the old theme: a custom WordPress theme from scratch with a new visual system, editorial fields for featured placement, and ad zones wired into the layout from day one.

Content would be organized by category and by automotive brand, not only by publish date. The plan included a curated homepage, category hubs, a multimedia gallery for OEM campaigns, and automated social distribution to grow engagement without manual reposting.

On the platform side: archive migration into the new model, a dedicated server, domains, email, and ongoing hosting and maintenance under WALOO.

Results

Performance, reach & discoverability

After relaunch, desktop Lighthouse moved from roughly 50 to 95. The homepage went from ~25 seconds in the field to a 3.2s full load in lab tests—DOMContentLoaded from 10.89s to 4.42s, full page load from 11.50s to 3.2s.

Alexa positioning improved as traffic grew: global rank climbed by +250,587 to ~1.26M worldwide; in Argentina the site reached #807.

Combined with structured publishing, editorial SEO, and social auto-distribution, Tiempo Motor began appearing on the first page of Google for searches tied to the national automotive news market. Editors gained a homepage they could curate without developers; the commercial team had ad-ready zones and multimedia inventory for OEM campaigns.
