Services

Theme implementation

Commercial and custom WordPress themes tuned to how you publish—lean templates, fewer plugins, and performance guardrails before launch.

Themes that match how you actually publish

We take existing WordPress themes—commercial, agency-built, or in-house—and align them with your brand, content model, and performance targets without a full rebuild when a rebuild is not justified.

We audit template hierarchy, plugin footprint, and editor workflows, then deliver a prioritized backlog: what to customize, what to standardise, and what to remove. The goal is a maintainable theme your team can run after handoff, not a demo that collapses under real catalog or campaign load.

Audit before customization

We map page types, plugin dependencies, and editor pain points so customization spend goes where it changes outcomes—not every optional widget the theme ships with.

Template overrides, child-theme structure, and documentation spell out what is safe for your team to change versus what needs a developer.

Lean templates, not theme bloat

We strip unused styles, scripts, and demo content patterns that inflate weight without helping conversion or editorial workflow.

Critical URLs get before/after weight checks; we prefer native blocks and theme APIs over stacking page builders when the brief allows.

Editor-ready handoff

Field groups, block patterns, and guardrails match how marketing and legal actually publish—fewer one-off page builds after go-live.

We train your leads on the publishing model and leave a short playbook so launches and seasonal updates stay predictable.

How we deliver

What you get on every engagement

Theme work includes measurable cleanup, documented ownership, and release discipline—not cosmetic tweaks alone.

Performance-aware customization

Every script and stylesheet change is weighed against Core Web Vitals and real-device testing on templates that carry traffic.

We avoid customization patterns that look fine in a staging screenshot but fail under production caching, ads, and merchandising plugins.

Brand alignment without rework

Typography, spacing, and component states are brought in line with your design system within the constraints of the parent theme.

Where the theme fights the brand, we document the trade-off and recommend scoped overrides versus fighting the framework.

Governance for future releases

We define what theme updates are safe to apply, what to regression-test, and what requires our review before click-to-update.

That governance protects the performance win after the first agency or internal team touches the site.
